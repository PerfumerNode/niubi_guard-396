# Code of Conduct

Niubi Guard exists to help maintainers handle abuse without creating new abuse.

Contributors are expected to be respectful, assume good faith, and keep discussions focused on reproducible behavior, maintainability, and user safety. Harassment, targeted insults, doxxing, spam, or coordinated pressure campaigns are not acceptable in project spaces.

Maintainers may hide, edit, or remove comments and may restrict participation when needed to keep the project usable for contributors and maintainers.
